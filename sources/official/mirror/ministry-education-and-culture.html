      Ministry of Education, Culture, Youth Affairs and Sports

      The Ministry provides leadership and funding, develops policy and legislation, develops accountability frameworks and monitors performance for all education, youth, sports, library, cultural and rehabilitative matters.

      The Ministry of Education, Culture, Youth Affairs and Sports has responsibility for all educational services, the Department of Culture, the Department of Library Services, the Department of Youth Affairs and Sports as well as the BVI National Commission for UNESCO.

      Vision

      To develop the mental, physical, social and spiritual being of every individual whom the Ministry serves so that they become responsible and productive in an increasingly vibrant and technological Virgin Islands society.

      Mission

      To provide high quality education and rehabilitative services, as well as, to facilitate access to and participation in sports, recreation and culture, in order to enrich the lives of all residents of these Virgin Islands.

      Core Values

      We believe in a safe and healthy working environment where employees feel valued and respected, where confidentiality and ethics are promoted, and efficient service is rendered.
